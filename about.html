Erin was born in Providence Rhode Island in 1991 at Woman and Infants Hospital. He grew up mostly in and around Providence RI until he was a freshman in High School where he moved to Hollis New Hampshire. Hollis is a small New Hampshire farming town with a population size of approximately 7000, which is much different than the city of Providence which has a population of 190,000. This was a nice change for Erin as he felt like he had better educational opportunities in Hollis. His favorite class was English because you could express yourself through writing.

Erin started his career by finding a cook position at St. Joseph’s Hospital in Nashua New Hampshire. He found the position through Project Search, a program preparing young people with disabilities for successful employment. Erin excelled in this program and gave many speeches on his success to aspiring program members. Erin is currently a Food Prep Cook and he works the FLIK company which provides corporate dining for one of its clients, Fidelity Investments, in Merrimack New Hampshire.

Erin’s hobbies include watching and playing basketball as well as playing video games. Erin’s favorite basketball team is the Boston Celtics. His favorite player is Jason Tatum because he is a good three-point shooter and he is an excellent role model. Erin’s favorite video game is Super Mario Brothers. He enjoys playing this game because you can gain points for achievements while playing the game. His favorite part is when he beats the game which he finds this very satisfying. Erin also likes to travel, although he admits he has not been many places. The most rememberable one was a trip to Los Angeles California. He hopes to take similar trips in the future.
